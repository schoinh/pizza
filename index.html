Bagheera's Pizzeria

Making You and Your Cats' Pizza Dreams Come True Since 2018

Welcome to Bagheera's Pizzeria, a pet-friendly destination for pizza lovers. Our pizzas are made with vegetables and meat straight from our farm. If we don't grow it ourselves, we source local and organic whenever possible.

In our kitchen, each ingredient is carefully inspected and the creation of each pizza closely supervised by Bagheera Choi, a 2-year-old black cat with a most discerning palate. Each finished creation is taste-tested in the kitchen by Caramel Choi, a 1-year-old brown tabby cat who will eat literally anything is the winner of the prestigious 2019 Feline Foodie Award.

Stop in today and taste for yourself why humans and non-humans alike call our pizzas the CAT'S MEOW.
